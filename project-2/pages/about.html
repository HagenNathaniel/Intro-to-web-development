About the Author

For close to 19 years, I have been an avid computer user, specializing in PC hardware technology. As a set of side hobbies, I am a Twitch streamer, content creator, coder, gamer, PC builder and a MASSIVE nerd. I enjoy all things nerdy, geeky, and electronic, and also LOVE fantasy art!

In 2014, I began my college pursuits at the University of Montana, Bitterroot College program. In early 2017, I graduated with an AAS in Information technology, with an AS in Computer Support.

In late 2017, I began my second major degree in the University of Montana, first starting at the Missoula College campus, than moving to the mountain campus in fall of 2018. I am currently 33 years old. The degree I am working on, is a Bachelor's in Fine Arts, Media Arts, with a focus on Digital art and technology.
